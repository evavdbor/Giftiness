FAQ

Your frequently asked questions

Have a question? Before sending us an email, please have a look at these frequently asked questions. Still want to ask your question? Just send us an email!

What is 'Giftiness' exactly?

Giftiness, helps you find a gift for your loved one. There are hundreds of gift websites out there and sometimes you just don't have the time to search through them all for that right gift. That's where we come in, based on the questioned you answered we look on different websites to find you that gift.

Why Giftiness?

We're not some large corporate business, we think about you and what's right for your giftee. We spend time browsing the internet for that right gift. Our goal is to make giving presents fun again!

What does it cost?

Giftiness is currently totally free! Please enjoy our service and we promise we won't charge you :).

Do you deliver the gifts?

No we don't. We just help you find the right gift, but it's up to you to order it. In your suggestion list, every item will have a 'Buy now' button. Just click that and you'll be directed to the right website.

Why do I need to fill out the questionnaire?

With the questionnaire, we try and figure out the person you need a gift for. It's hard to find a gift for someone you don't know, so we need some guidance from you. We've narrowed the questionnaire down to a couple of not too personal details. These will give us an image of the person you need a gift for.

In which countries can I use Giftiness?

At the moment we're only looking for gifts in the UK. In the future we will be able to provide more international personalised gift suggestions.

I haven't received my suggestions yet.

First of all, we're sorry you haven't received your suggestions yet. Just remember, Giftiness is just me, Eva looking for the perfect gift, so it might take a bit longer to find it. If this is time-sensitive just send us another email and we'll be sure to reply asap.

Your suggestions - it's just not what I wanted

We're sorry to hear that we didn't find exactly what you were looking for. Do reach out to us and we're happy to give it another go!

How do I get started?

Easy! Just click on 'Find a Gift' in the navbar on the top of the page. You'll be directed to the questionnaire, just fill that out and we'll get searching.

How do I contact you?

Just click the button below and send us an email! Giftinessuk[at]gmail.com is the best way to reach us.
