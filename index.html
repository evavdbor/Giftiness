About

Hi I'm Eva!

I've been buying my grandparents' christmas gifts for the grandkids for the last 10 years and this is how I started to love finding the perfect gift for someone. When I moved to London, buying gifts for my family and friends just became harder to organise. Surfing to local websites, ordering to my UK address and then lugging 5 suitcases home to Belgium (which is not even that far) on the Eurostar. This made me think about an easier system to find and buy gifts and from this Giftiness was born! - HURRAY!-
